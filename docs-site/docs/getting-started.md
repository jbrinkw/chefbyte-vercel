## Core Workflow

ChefByte follows a logical flow from products to recipes to meal plans to tracking.

### 1. Build Your Inventory

Start by adding products to your inventory:

- **Barcode Scanning**: Use the Scanner page to scan product barcodes
- **Manual Entry**: Create products in Settings then Products
- **AI Enhancement**: Scanned products automatically get nutrition data

### 2. Create Recipes

Build recipes using your inventory:

- Add ingredients with amounts
- Macros calculate automatically
- Set prep and cook times

### 3. Plan Your Meals

Use the Meal Plan page to:

- Assign recipes to specific days
- Toggle meal prep mode for batch cooking
- See daily macro totals

### 4. Track Your Progress

The dashboard shows:

- Today's macro progress (calories, protein, carbs, fats)
- Meals completed vs planned
- Quick actions for common tasks

### 5. Shop for What You Need

When running low:

- Click Add Below Min Stock to auto-populate shopping list
- Use Meal Plan to Cart to add recipe ingredients
- Export to Walmart for price-optimized shopping

## Setting Your Macro Goals

Before tracking, configure your targets:

1. Go to **Home** page
2. Click **Target Macros**
3. Enter your goals:
   - Carbs (grams)
   - Protein (grams)
   - Fats (grams)
4. Calories calculate automatically using the 4-4-9 rule

!!! tip "Calculating Goals"
    A common starting point: Protein at 0.8-1g per pound of body weight, Fats at 25-35 percent of total calories, and Carbs to fill remaining calories.
